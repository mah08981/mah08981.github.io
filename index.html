About

Bangles by Mollie is a jewelry company started in 2014 in Royston, Georgia by Mollie Herndon. Frustrated with outrageous prices for jewelry in local boutiques, Mollie decided to learn how to make jewelry herself. Mollie began by making wire bangles, and has now expanded to bracelets, necklaces, rings, chokers and more! Every piece is unique and handcrafted to perfection, with intricate designs and extreme attention to detail. Bangles by Mollie was created with a mission to deliver trendy, one-of-a-kind pieces of jewelry without the high boutique price tag.

About the Designer

Mollie Herndon began Bangles by Mollie as a junior in high school, and still makes jewelry today as a freshman in college at the University of Georgia. What began as a small hobby has expanded into her own business, which includes selling her pieces in local stores. Mollie's jewelry-making skills are self-taught, which creates a truly unique product.

Our Products

Bangles by Mollie has a variety of goodies to choose from, including long, beaded necklaces, edgy chokers, wire-wrapped bangles & rings, beaded stretch-bracelets, earrings, and even key-chains! Every piece is handcrafted and can be customized to your personal style. Bangles by Mollie is sold at Dafodill Hill in Danielsville, Georgia as well as at Blue Haven Bee Co. in Canon, Georgia. Mollie also accepts customized orders.
